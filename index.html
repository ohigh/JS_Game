WELKOME to A-MAZE-ING GAME

Rules:

You have 30 seconds to get through the maze and reach the treasure chest.

- You get points for every move on the path.

- You get extra points if you collect diamonds and keys, if you go through the tunnel and if you reach the treasure chest.

- You lose points if you hit the wall.

- You die if you hit a bomb or fail to reach the treasure chest within 30 seconds.

- Enjoy!
